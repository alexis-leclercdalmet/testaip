Our elected students in the UFR Councils

In addition to the Central Councils, the University is divided into training and research units (UFR):

  • UFR Capacity and 1st cycle of law and political science - 4/12 student seats;
  • UFR 2nd and 3rd cycles of law and political science - 3/9 student seats;
  • UFR Economic and Management Sciences - not candidates;
  • UFR Private and public management sciences - not candidates;
  • UFR French Press Institute - 1/3 student seats;
  • UFR Institute of Judicial Studies - 4/4 student seats.
